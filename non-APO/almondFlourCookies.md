# Almond flour (lemon) cookies

## Ingredients
* 112 g almond flour
* Up to 30 grams of sugar/sweetener; less is fine
* 1/2 tsp baking powder
* 1/8 teaspoon salt
* 2-3 Tb water

Optional, but for added deliciousness: 
* lemon extract
* vanilla extract
* poppy seeds

## Directions
1. Combine ingredients. Start with 2 Tb water, add more if necessary.
